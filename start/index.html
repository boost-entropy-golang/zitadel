Try ZITADEL

You can either use ZITADEL.ch or deploy a dedicated ZITADEL instance.

Use ZITADEL.ch

To register your free organisation, visit this link register organisation. After accepting the TOS and filling out all the required fields you will receive an email with further instructions.

Verify your domain name (optional)

When you verify your domain you get the benefit that your organisations users can use this domain as preferred logonname. You find a more detailed explanation How ZITADEL handles usernames.

The verification process is documented here
